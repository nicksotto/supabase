---
title: 'Rate limits'
subtitle: 'Rate limits protect your services from abuse'
---

Supabase Auth enforces rate limits on endpoints to prevent abuse. Some rate limits are [customizable](/dashboard/project/_/auth/rate-limits). Note that rate limits continue to apply when:
- Email confirmations or Phone Confirmations are disabled
- Send Email or Send SMS Hook is in use.
